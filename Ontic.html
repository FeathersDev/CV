IT Support Analyst

This is a brief job Description of my role at Ontic Engineering and Manufacturing.

Ontic Engineering and Manufacturing support and repair legacy aircraft that are at the end of their support life. Their sites are all over the world including the UK, America and Singapore. There are approximately 350 staff members, with my role as front line support being the face of the IT Team. The below list of bullet points gives an idea of my day to role and abilities:

  • Log tickets using Service Now.
  • Diagnosing and resolving issues where possible before escalation if needed.
  • Communicating through phone calls, emails, Teams and walk-ups.
  • Daily walk around to ensure that users are happy and troubleshoot if needed. This helps promote trust between IT and the rest of business.
  • Remote working through Teams to home users
  • Configuration of laptops, desktops and Iphone's.
  • Installation of business software.
  • Use of Citrix Receiver, Manufacturing software from Infor, Office 365.
  • Working from home, weekends (on-site) and extra hours in the day to cover demands (on-site and at home).
  • Hardware configuration: Laptops, desktops, patch panel.
