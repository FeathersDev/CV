IT Technical Support

This is a brief job Description of my role at Gloucestershire Engineering Training.

Gloucestershire Engineering Training is a privately run apprenticeship provider for the engineering industry. Over 60 staff members are employed, along with over 400 learners that attend Gloucestershire Engineering Training and approximately 250 on-site at any given time. I worked as the front-line IT support and reported to the senior IT Technical Office and our Line Manager for the Business Services Team. Below is a bullet point guide to my role and abilities.

  • Installing and configuring Windows 10.
  • Identifying and resolving hardware and software faults.
  • Basic networking knowledge.
  • Upgrading hardware and software.
  • Support for Multifunctional Devices.
  • Setting up new accounts in Active Directory.
  • Basic Group Policy.
  • Introductions to new employees and learners about IT policies.
  • Team meetings and meetings with department managers.
  • Liaising and purchasing software and hardware from external vendors.
  • Creating procedural documents and process maps.
  • Helping to track and maintain the IT budget.
  • Problem solving.
